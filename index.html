About Our Technology

Our fake news detection system uses advanced natural language processing algorithms to analyze news content for signs of misinformation. We evaluate multiple factors including:

  • Language patterns and emotional manipulation
  • Source credibility and reputation
  • Fact verification against trusted databases
  • Statistical outliers in presentation

We're committed to promoting media literacy and helping everyone make more informed choices about the news they consume and share.
